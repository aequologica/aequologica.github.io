### 1. [Ctrl-shift-click me ](http://aequologica.net/x_et_thiebaud/tool.html "æquologica's Soundcloud Commentor") to launch æquologica's Soundcloud Commentor in a new tab ### 2. A popup window, well ... pops up. (you probably need to allow the browser to pop the thing up) ![Soundcloud login popup](00_soundcloud_login_popup.jpg "Soundcloud login popup") Please allow aequologica to access your soundcloud account. Don't worry, I won't do anything wrong with it. I will only post the comments you'll ask me to post with your user ID. ### 3. Ok, I found you (1) ! Please paste the soundcloud URL of [the track you want to comment](https://soundcloud.com/christophe-thiebaud/cest-pas-beau-a-voir "«c est-pas-beau-a-voir » infamous playground track") (2) and click the magnifying glass (3). ![Search soundcloud](01_after_login_search.jpg "Search soundcloud") ### 4. Ok, I found the track (1) ! Please enter (better, paste) the text you want to synchronize as soundcloud comments (2) and push it to the left (3). ![Enter text](02_after_search_enter_text.jpg "Enter text") ### 5. Text is pushed (1), ready to start syncing ! Press the play button of the soundcloud widget (2). ![Text pushed](03_after_push_text.jpg "Text pushed") ### 6. Click on each row of the left text in sync with the track ![Click to sync](04_sync.jpg "Click to sync") The text is striked through (1), so that you won't insert it twice by mistake, and the timestamp in milliseconds is displayed on the right (2) ![After click to sync](05_after_sync.jpg "After click to sync") ### 7. When you're done, click 'post to sound cloud' (1). Each comment will be inserted in reverse order (2), waiting about one second between each inserts, so that the comments are ordered nicely in the soundcloud web application (which displays most recent comments first). ![Starting insertion](06_start_insertion.jpg "Starting insertion") When all inserts are done ... ![Insertion finished](07_insertion_finished.jpg "Insertion finished") ... you can verify the results on [the soundcloud track](https://soundcloud.com/christophe-thiebaud/cest-pas-beau-a-voir "«c est-pas-beau-a-voir » infamous playground track") : ![Results on soundcloud](08_results_on_soundcloud.jpg "Results on soundcloud") ### 8. finally, lat but not least, a dedicate « karaoké » web page has been generated at the following URL https://tebaldi051108trial.hanatrial.ondemand.com/paroles/iframe?trackId=200813526&amp;userId=17366398 FYI, this very URL is displayed just below the soundcloud player : ![Results on soundcloud](09_karaoke.jpg "Results on soundcloud") This url can be embedded in an iframe in another site, e.g. [here](http://aequologica.net/donutsfm_et_thiebaud/iframe.html) with only [very little coding](https://github.com/aequologica/aequologica.github.io/blob/master/donutsfm_et_thiebaud/iframe.html) : ```html <!DOCTYPE HTML> <html lang="fr"> <head> <title>Madame la vache</title> </head> <body> <div>attention, ça va arriver . . . </div> <iframe id="aequologica-iframe" width="100%" height="800px" frameborder="no" src="https://tebaldi051108trial.hanatrial.ondemand.com/paroles/iframe?trackId=68030959&amp;userId=154105433"></iframe> </body> </html> ``` ### 9.
